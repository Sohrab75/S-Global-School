overview

Isabellas International School - A seat of ECCE, focusing on Holistic development and Experiential Learning and promoting Technology based Curriculum.

Isabellas International School is a recognized school, grooming Foundational years of Learning, with an objective to provide quality and fun merged learning experience, for the tiny tots. It takes at least a year to prepare a child for the formal school which makes Foundational years of Education extremely crucial.It lays the foundation of schooling for your child, thus making education a joyful and an enriching experience. In The City of Pune we offer a lush green eco-friendly educational environment for children (Play group – 7th STD)

Isabellas International employs a fully proven methodology, to build a robust value system among the students, so that they may eventually transform into responsible and cultured future denizens of the nation. Students are nurtured with complete exposure to experiential curriculum so that they radiate self-confidence and exhibit a fearless approach during public address and stage performances. Our children develop strong executive functions, a love and bond with nature along with a sense of belonging and commitment towards the community.
